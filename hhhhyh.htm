SPORTS

Sports can be defined as an activity involving physical exertion and skill in which an individual or team competes against another or others for entertainment.

different types of sport

There are 3 types of sports:

  • individual sports
  • dual sports
  • team sports

Examples of sports

Basketball: a game played between two teams of five players in which goals are scored by throwing a ball through a netted hoop fixed at each end of the court.

Tennis: a game in which two or four players strike a ball with rackets over a net stretched across a court.
